“It is more blessed to give than to receive.”


― The Bible(Acts 20:35)
A charity is an organization which raises money in order to help people who are sick or very poor, or who have a disability. Charity is done for the benefit, assistance, and relief of people in need. When carried out selflessly, it is a one-way act where an individual gives and asks for nothing in return. We forget that true happiness is beyond the definitions of owning and possessing and that it is the spiritual consciousness of defining various moments in our lives with love, grace, and gratitude.
